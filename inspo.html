Alumni

  • Madame Ghandi

    Madame Gandhi is an LA-based artist and activist known for her uplifting, percussive electronic music and positive message about gender liberation and personal power. She came to IO to further develop her workflow inside Ableton Live.

  • Travis Emmons

    One of our very first graduates, Travis unique brand of high-energy house music has earned him chart-topping success with multiple Beatport number ones on labels such as Glasgow Underground, Mobilee, Farris Wheel, Brock Wild, Weapons (UK), Perfect Driver, House of Hustle, and many more. Spot Travis on lineups around Los Angeles and beyond.

  • Masha Mar

    Dig Deeper and Surround co-founder, Masha has played everything from Boiler Room to Coachella. On the production side, her first release came out on SOS Records via K7 and her debut vinyl release will be an original EP on BOUQUET.

  • Mary Droppinz

    While already an upcoming star behind the decks when we met her, Alyssa came to us ready to take her production skills to the next level. Since her time at IO, she has gone on to release music on Insomniacs Discovery Project, Altered States, and POPGANG. You can catch her playing at festivals such as Desert Hearts and Coachella or on tour with Sofi Tukker.

  • SOOHAN

    Long known within the festival community as a skilled performer, remixer, and producer, Maryland's SOOHAN (Stephen Suchan) came to IO ready to dive deeper into music theory and musicianship. Check out SOOHAN on the lineups of upcoming festivals such as Lucidity and Northern Nights.
